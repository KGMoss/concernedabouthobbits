Concerned About Hobbits

FOTR

Howard Shore's award-winning Lord of the Rings scores are renowned for their beauty, emotion, and detail. Specifically, it's Shore's thematic writing that makes LOTR's music so powerful, as well as so true to Tolkien's work. This style of writing allows the score to function almost like an opera, as Shore's usage of leitmotifs reveals a sort of story behind the story. In this visual essay, I explore Shore's thematic storytelling in the extended edition of The Fellowship of the Ring.
